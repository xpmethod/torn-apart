What we know now: Police say a 14-year-old was raped outside York Fair

Joel Shannon and Teresa Boeckel, York Daily Record Published 11:18 a.m. ET Nov. 22, 2017 | Updated 4:42 p.m. ET Nov. 22, 2017

West York Policesay three teen boys raped a 14-year-old girl after leaving the York Fair. Here's what we know about the alleged assault:

The latest:

Full story: Teen boys raped 14-year-old girl after leaving York Fair, West York Police say

  • Three 16-year-old boys from Manchester Township have been charged as adults with rape - forcible compulsion, involuntary deviate sexual intercourse, unlawful restraint and terroristic threats.
  • Their names: Kelvin J. Mercedes of the 2500 block of North George Street, Andrew R. Miller of the 600 block of Norman Road, and Daishon M. Richardson of the 1000 block of Hearthridge Lane.
  • The suspects were all members of the Central York High School football team.
  • As of Wednesday afternoon, the three had posted $25,000 bail each, but were waiting at York County Prison to speak with probation on the conditions of their supervised bail.
  • West York Police Detective Michael Mendez Sr., the lead investigating officer, said the girl knew the teens before the incident.
  • Julie Randall Romig, director of communications for Central York School District, said Wednesday in an email, "Law enforcement officials have advised that this is a non-school related issue and, therefore, we do not have any comment on the ongoing police investigation. At no time were any students, faculty or staff in danger on school property as a result of this non-school related incident."

Whathappened, according to police:

CLOSE

West York police detective Michael Mendez, talks about the three teens charged with raping a 14-year-old girl they lured from the York Fair on Sept. 16. York Daily Record

The assault occurred on September 17, the last day of the York Fair, according to a search warrant for the case from West York Borough Police.

Police interviewed the victim on Sept.27, 2017, according toan affidavit of probable cause.

Full story: Teen boys raped 14-year-old girl after leaving York Fair, West York Police say

Here is the timeline of events,according to the affidavit:

The assault

CLOSE

Three 16-year-old boys from Manchester Township have been charged with raping a 14-year-old girl at gunpoint after leaving the York Fair. Sean Heisey, York Daily Record

  • The victim attended the fair with a friend and met up with twogroups of friends.
  • Mercedes,Miller andRichardson followed the victim and her friends. They "told her that they wanted toshow her something and for her to follow them."
  • After leaving the fair, the victim said she saw a gun displayed byMercedes.
  • The group moved between multiple locations during the assault. They left one business close to the fair after seeing police. The group assaulted the girl in a parking lot and in an unknown yard with a well.
  • The victim said Mercedes put a gun to her head, and she was afraid she would be killed if she did not do as they said. She said the gun had an extended magazine.
  • She was forced to perform oral sex on all three suspects, the affidavit states.
  • She was sexually assaulted at another location, the affidavit states. The males "took turns holding her down."
  • The victim said the assault was recorded on video and/or photographed.
  • After the assault, the victim was placeda maroon SUV. "She stated that they were being 'aggressive with her' at that point," the affidavit states.
